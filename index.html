Overview

Academic depth with applied engineering impact

What I work on

I study advanced statistical learning methods for quality, reliability, and prognostics. My interests include Bayesian modeling, Gaussian processes, survival analysis, physics-informed neural networks, and Bayesian last-layer neural networks.

How I position my work

I care about models that respect engineering structure, communicate uncertainty clearly, and remain usable by decision-makers in noisy, real-world environments. That balance between rigor and usefulness defines the kind of research I want to build.
